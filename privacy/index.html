Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NGP Hub. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use our services, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

By using NGP Hub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username/display name
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Account creation date and login history

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our services:

  • IP address and general location (city/state level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and features used
  • Referral source (how you found us)
  • Error logs and performance data

AI Interaction Data: When you use Nirvana AI:

  • Conversation history and prompts
  • AI-generated responses
  • Interaction timestamps and session duration
  • User feedback and ratings

Payment Information: If you subscribe to paid services, we collect:

  • Billing information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Subscription tier and status
  • Transaction history

Note: We do not store full credit card numbers. Payment processing is handled by secure third-party services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve NGP Hub services including tools, games, and AI features
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process subscriptions, and verify your identity
  • AI Improvement: To train, test, and improve Nirvana AI's performance, accuracy, and capabilities
  • Communication: To send you service updates, security alerts, and respond to your inquiries
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our services and identify areas for improvement
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, security incidents, and other harmful activities
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Bug Fixes: To diagnose technical issues and fix bugs in our services

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (Firebase, payment processors, hosting providers) that may access your data to help us operate our services
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: We may share information to protect the rights, property, or safety of NGP, our users, or the public
  • Business Transfers: If NGP is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing

Important: AI conversations may be used to improve Nirvana's capabilities, but we do not share identifiable user data with third parties for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after account closure
  • AI Conversations: May be retained indefinitely for AI training and improvement purposes, but may be anonymized
  • Usage Logs: Typically retained for 90 days to 2 years for analytics and security purposes
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by law for tax and accounting purposes (typically 7 years)

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Note that some data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications (though we send very few)
  • Restriction: You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact page. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

California Residents: Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have additional rights including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information).

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

NGP Hub is intended for users aged 13 and older. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Parents and guardians: If your child is between 13-17 and using our services, we encourage you to monitor their activity and discuss online safety with them.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours via email and/or through a notice on our website.

13. Yoad Discord Bot Data Practices

When you interact with "Yoad," our Discord bot, additional data collection and processing occurs specific to the bot's functionality. This section details what data Yoad collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding this data.

Data Collected by Yoad:

  • Message Content: All messages sent in channels where Yoad is present are accessible to the bot for command processing and AI response generation. Message content is logged in the bot operator's terminal for debugging and monitoring purposes.
  • Discord Identifiers: User IDs, usernames, discriminators, server/guild IDs, channel IDs, and message IDs are collected to identify interactions and provide bot services.
  • Voice Channel Audio: When "hearing mode" is enabled, Yoad receives voice channel audio streams in real-time. This audio is played back through the bot operator's system but is NOT permanently recorded or stored to disk.
  • AI Conversation History: Interactions with YoadGPT and ChatGPT features are stored to maintain conversation context and improve AI model performance. These conversations include your prompts and the AI's responses.
  • Interaction Metadata: Timestamps, command usage, reaction additions, and other bot interaction data are logged for analytics and debugging.
  • Image Previews: When you send image attachments in channels where Yoad is present, these images may be previewed in the bot operator's terminal interface for monitoring purposes. Images are not permanently stored by the bot.
  • Error Logs: Technical errors, exceptions, and bot performance data are logged to help identify and fix issues.

How Yoad Data is Used:

  • Service Provision: To execute commands, play audio, generate AI responses, and provide bot functionality
  • AI Training & Improvement: Conversation history with Yoad's AI features may be used to fine-tune and improve OpenAI models and the bot's response quality
  • Debugging & Monitoring: Message logs and error reports help bot operators identify issues, fix bugs, and maintain bot stability
  • Feature Development: Usage patterns and interaction data inform decisions about new features and improvements
  • Security & Abuse Prevention: Logs help identify misuse, spam, or attempts to exploit the bot

Data Retention for Yoad:

  • Message Logs: Terminal logs are retained during active bot sessions and may be saved to log files at the bot operator's discretion. These logs may be retained indefinitely for debugging purposes.
  • AI Conversation History: Stored in memory during bot runtime and may be persisted to maintain conversation context across sessions. May be retained indefinitely for AI training purposes.
  • Voice Audio: Real-time only - not stored or recorded permanently
  • Error Logs: Retained for debugging purposes, typically for 30-90 days

Data Sharing:

  • OpenAI: AI prompts and responses are processed by OpenAI's API. OpenAI's privacy policy and data practices apply to this data. As of our last update, OpenAI does not use API data for training unless you explicitly opt in.
  • Discord: Yoad operates through Discord's platform and must comply with Discord's API Terms. Discord has access to all bot interactions as part of their platform.
  • Bot Operators: NGP team members who operate Yoad can see message logs, user interactions, and bot activity through the terminal interface.

Your Rights Regarding Yoad Data:

  • Removal from Servers: Server administrators can remove Yoad from their server at any time, which prevents further data collection from that server
  • Data Deletion Requests: You may request deletion of your AI conversation history or other Yoad-related data by contacting us. Note that some terminal logs may have been archived and cannot be fully deleted.
  • Opt-Out of AI Features: Server administrators can restrict which channels Yoad can access, limiting data collection to specific channels

Important Considerations:

  • Server administrators are responsible for informing their members about Yoad's presence and data practices
  • By using Discord servers where Yoad is present, you acknowledge that the bot may log your messages and interactions
  • Yoad's "Chaos Mode" feature may cause the bot to randomly interact with your messages (replies, reactions) without being directly invoked
  • Voice channel audio in "hearing mode" is transmitted in real-time to bot operators but is not recorded or stored permanently
  • Avoid sharing sensitive personal information in channels where Yoad is present, as message content may be logged

For questions specific to Yoad's data practices, to request data deletion, or to report concerns, please contact us through our Discord server or contact page.
