    NGP-SMP Complete Server Rules

    NGP-SMP Complete Rules Last updated: March 30, 2026

    Server Information

    Server IP ngp-smp.playit.plus
    Recommended Version 1.21.x (any version works except April Fools updates)
    Voice Chat Simple Voice Chat (Recommended but not required)

    Welcome to NGP-SMP! This is a survival multiplayer server with lore elements, a player-driven economy, and strict rules to ensure fair gameplay for everyone. Please read ALL rules carefully before joining the server.

    IMPORTANT

    Violating these rules will result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server. Staff decisions are final. Don't try to be clever - we will catch you.

    Server Rules

    1. Respect & Conduct

    • No disrespect, harassment, or abusive behavior toward other players
    • No offensive language, hate speech, racism, or discrimination
    • No sharing personal information about others
    • No spamming or rioting
    • Discord server rules still apply and may be enforced here
    • Discussion of politics, religion, or NSFW topics is allowed but must be kept minimal and respectful - don't overdo it

    2. Griefing & Property

    • No griefing other players' builds - modifying, destroying, or altering someone's build without permission is classified as griefing
    • No chest tampering - this includes taking items out and putting them back in the chest, or messing with chest orders and reorganizing someone's storage
    • No stealing from other players
    • No breaking blocks on another player's land without permission
    • No killing mobs on another player's land without permission
    • Trespassing - if asked to leave someone's land, you must leave immediately. However, base owners may NOT ban or punish someone within 2 seconds just because they're on their property - give them reasonable time to leave if they're clearly on their way out
    • Terrain modifications - minor changes to natural terrain (like placing a grass block on grass) are fine unless it's directly in or around someone's base
    • No prank wars of any kind
    • No redstone lag machines - intentional or accidental

    3. PvP & Combat

    • No random PvP on strangers or people you don't know well - killing random players for no reason is not allowed. However, killing friends as a joke in fair situations (like they said something dumb, playful banter, etc.) is fine as long as both parties are cool with it. Use common sense - don't be toxic to randoms.
    • Accidental kills are allowed if the victim forgives you
    • Wars are permitted if:
      • Both teams consent to the war
      • Teams are roughly equal in player count
      • Teams are roughly equal in gear and resources - both teams must have the capability to fight fairly
    • World Wars (WW) can ONLY be declared by top-tier admins
      • WW will be announced in the announcements channel with a proper ping
      • During WW, no base is safe unless hidden - everyone will fight to the death throughout every team
      • Keep Inventory MAY be disabled during WW (will be specified in the announcement)
      • All players fight across all teams - total warfare

    4. Accounts & Bans

    • No alternate accounts under any circumstances - this also goes under NGP Standards
    • No ban evasion using alternate accounts
    • Ban Appeals: Do NOT appeal bans in General chat - create a ticket if you feel you've been unbanned unfairly

    5. Prohibited Content

    • No inappropriate builds or symbols - this isn't too strict unless it's anything racist
    • No inappropriate account names and skins
    • No advertising other servers or IPs
    • No trading in-game items for real money and vice versa - advertising and trading in-game items for real money is strictly prohibited

    6. Cheating & Exploits

    Prohibited Clients & Mods

    • No X-Ray texture packs, mods, or resource packs
    • No hacked clients - Wurst, LiquidBounce, Aristois, Impact, etc.
    • No cheat clients of any kind - this includes "utility" clients
    • No Baritone or any pathfinding/automation bots
    • No killaura, reach, or combat hacks
    • No fly hacks or speed hacks
    • No freecam mods that allow you to see through walls
    • No ESP/Tracers - player/mob/chest tracking through walls
    • No auto-miners, auto-fishers, or AFK bots
    • No inventory/chest sorters that auto-steal
    • No scaffold/tower hacks
    • No nuker/breaker mods

    Allowed Mods

    • Full Bright / Gamma mods - ONLY visual brightness enhancement is allowed
    • Optifine / Sodium / Performance mods - FPS boosting only
    • Minimap mods (JourneyMap, Xaero's) - as long as they don't show caves/players through walls
    • Inventory management (Mouse Tweaks, Inventory Tweaks) - organizing only, NO auto-actions
    • Shaders - visual enhancement only
    • Cosmetic mods - capes, emotes, etc.
    • Voice chat mods - Simple Voice Chat is built into the server!
    • Replay Mod - recording only

    IF YOU'RE UNSURE IF A MOD IS ALLOWED

    Ask staff BEFORE using it! Better safe than banned.

    Exploits & Glitches

    • No duplication glitches of any kind
    • No chunk loaders that bypass server limits
    • No exploiting game mechanics for unfair advantages - bedrock breaking, etc.
    • No lag machines or intentional server crashes

    Illegal Enchantments

    • Items with enchantments above their vanilla max level are LEGAL if purchased from the server economy
    • Example: Knockback V (max is II) can cost $500,000+ but is allowed if bought legitimately through the server economy
    • Obtaining overpowered enchants through glitches/cheats is NOT allowed

    7. Economy & Farming Rules

    Automated Farming Restrictions

    • You MAY NOT farm any item worth $100 or more according to the /worth command
    • This includes enderman farms (enderpearls worth $100+), wither skeleton farms, or any other high-value mob/item farms
    • This rule applies even if you don't sell the items - just having the farm is illegal
    • If an item has no /worth value listed, DO NOT risk it - ask staff first before building a farm

    Selling & Trading Rules

    • Price splitting to bypass farming rules is ILLEGAL
      • Example ILLEGAL scheme: You cannot farm enderpearls ($100 each), sell them to a friend for $100, then have them resell for $150 and split the profits
      • Both the farmer AND the reseller will be permanently banned for this
    • Reselling items you legitimately obtained is allowed
      • You can buy low and sell high IF you didn't farm the items yourself
      • Price markup is allowed as long as the original source wasn't an illegal farm
    • If you're caught coordinating with someone to bypass the $100 farming rule through reselling schemes, both players will be permanently banned with no appeals

    What Counts as Farming?

    • Any automated system - mob farms, crop farms, mining setups, etc.
    • AFK grinding/killing for items
    • Using game mechanics to mass-produce high-value items
    • Manual grinding is allowed for items under $100 worth - actively playing and killing mobs yourself is fine

    Exceptions

    • Mining/gathering items worth less than $100 is always allowed
    • Manual trading and normal gameplay item collection is fine
    • Shops and player trading are encouraged, as long as items weren't illegally farmed

    Punishment Guidelines

    Punishments are issued based on severity and repeat offenses. Staff have discretion to adjust based on context.

    1. Respect & Conduct

    • First Offense: Warning or 1-7 day ban
    • Repeat Offense: 14-30 day ban
    • Severe/Repeated: Permanent ban

    2. Griefing & Property

    • Minor (accidental/first-time): Warning + rollback of the griefed area + possible money loss
    • Intentional: 7-30 day ban + rollback + money loss (up to all of your money)
    • Major/Repeated: Permanent ban + complete money wipe

    3. PvP & Combat

    • Accidental (unforgiven): Warning or 1-3 day ban
    • Intentional without consent: 7-14 day ban
    • Repeat offenses: 30 day to permanent ban

    4. Accounts & Bans

    • Alternate accounts: Permanent ban on all accounts
    • Ban evasion: Permanent ban, no appeals

    5. Prohibited Content

    • Minor violations: Warning or 1-7 day ban + content removal
    • Severe (racist/NSFW): Permanent ban

    6. Cheating & Exploits

    • First Offense: 7-30 day ban
    • Repeat/Severe: Permanent ban

    7. Economy & Farming

    • First Offense: 14-30 day ban + removal of farm/items + money reset
    • Bypassing via reselling schemes: Permanent ban for all involved players, no appeals
    • Repeat offenses: Permanent ban

    Additional Rules & Policies

    Server Owner Rights

    Server owners reserve the right to clear your inventory and items at any time for any reason. This can happen:

    • Upon request - if you want a fresh start
    • If staff determine you need to be reset due to duplication glitches, unfair advantages, etc.

    Server owners also have the right to:

    • Use /weather at any time if they have OP permissions
    • Use /give command on others if they lose their stuff, if they provide proof
    • Reorganize your inventory only if given permission from the user, and/or if they have to due to server rules/law
    • Use Spectator mode to fly around and observe the map
      • Server owners are NOT allowed to fly around the map to find hidden players
      • If they find hidden bases, they may NOT enter or go near it
      • OPs are NOT allowed to use Spectator mode to look for structures or hidden loot
    • Use Creative mode to fly around the map
      • The creative inventory is BANNED unless giving items to players under legal circumstances - if giving a player an item, use /give instead
      • May also be used to just not be in PvP, or just to watch stuff
      • May NOT trespass or grief while in Creative mode
    • Use WorldEdit if building something or messing around
      • May NOT grief bases with WorldEdit
      • Must ALWAYS do //undo if needed - they must always undo unless building an official lore structure
      • May NOT use WorldEdit to give themselves free stuff or anything
      • May also use WorldEdit + WorldGuard to make sure some monuments/buildings may never be griefed
      • May NOT use WorldEdit/WorldGuard to protect player bases - players must defend their own land
    • Give players random/joke items that don't significantly affect gameplay
      • Examples: dirt, anvils, barrels (but NOT chests), sticks, stone (but specifically NOT cobblestone), dead bushes, poisonous potatoes, signs, water bottles, and others that go along in those categories
      • This is for fun and does not break server balance

    Rollback Policy

    When griefing occurs, staff will restore the affected area to its original state before the damage happened.

    ENFORCEMENT NOTICE

    We will be stricter on rule enforcement for any violations that create unfair gameplay advantages. If a rule is being exploited to ruin the server balance or other players' experience, punishments will be more severe. Play fair, have fun, and don't be a dick.

    NOTE

    Extremely severe violations (doxxing, serious harassment, illegal content) result in instant permanent ban with no appeal. Staff decisions are final, but you can appeal most bans via ticket.

    Lore & Exploration

    Hidden Structures & Loot

    As you explore the map, you may discover ancient buildings marked by:

    • Heavy cobweb coverage
    • Spawners
    • Bedrock blocks
    • Hidden chests with loot

    Rules for Lore Structures

    • DO NOT claim or terraform these buildings - they are part of the server lore and must remain intact
    • DO NOT break spawners - leave them intact for others to discover
    • DO NOT break cobwebs unless they're directly blocking your path
    • You MAY take loot from chests - finders keepers! This is intentional
    • If you find books, READ them but PUT THEM BACK - do not take lore books with you. They're part of the story
    • These structures are intentionally placed content, not cheating or unfair advantages
    • Loot quality scales with world border expansion as players progress - the more the world border expands, the better the loot becomes because players get better
    • The lore: Most of the loot was placed from thousands of years ago from old travelers

    CAN'T TELL IF IT'S A PLAYER BASE OR LORE STRUCTURE?

    You will be able to tell the difference between a player's base and a lore structure. If you cannot tell whether something is a base or loot, either create a ticket or /w one of the mods to ask them for help!

    Rules for OPs Who Placed Loot

    • You CANNOT take your own loot unless:
      • Another player finds it while you were following them AND
      • They allow you to have a share, or all of it
    • This rule is strictly enforced - no exceptions whatsoever

    Questions?

    If you have questions about the rules or need clarification, create a ticket or ask in General!

    WELCOME TO NGP-SMP

    These rules exist to keep the server fair and fun for everyone. Follow them, respect other players, and have a great time exploring, building, and playing together!
