Roadmap

2021 — NGP Begins

A clan for a group of players that are naturally good at games.

2022 — A New Multiverse

Instead of only being on a VR game called "Gorilla Tag," we moved NGP across platforms.

2023 — A Change for the Better

The NGP clan made its first Discord server, reaching around 30 members (Flame doesn't remember the server name).

2024 — A Fall For Good

NGP as a whole started fading as people left to clear space, and events became less frequent. Even Flame dropped "NGP" from his name.

2024 Jun–Dec — A Thought

Flame realized NGP didn't have to stay a clan—it could become something else. He floated the idea in NGP-Hangout.

2025 Jan–Jul — And A Rise

Flame and Scrpt made NGP Studios. A few months later, Scrpt, Flame, and Hashtagg re-imagined the idea: turn NGP into a company. NGP began to rise again, with more new members joining daily.

2025 — Today

A real website, event work, positive community changes, and more utility-focused features… thanks to you.
