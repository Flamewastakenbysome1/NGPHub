About My GFX Work

Hey! I'm Flame, a Roblox GFX artist with years of experience creating high-quality graphics for profiles, games, and content creators. I specialize in character renders using Roblox Studio and professional editing software to deliver clean, professional results.

How I Work

  • Order Process: Contact me with your requirements, provide your Roblox username, and describe what you want
  • Customization: Tell me about poses, backgrounds, colors, and any specific details you need
  • Timeline: Most projects are completed within 3-48 hours depending on complexity
  • Revisions: I offer reasonable revisions to make sure you're happy with the final result
  • Delivery: Final files delivered in high quality PNG format via Discord or Roblox messages

What I Can Do

  • Roblox character renders in any pose or style
  • Custom backgrounds and scenes
  • Text overlays and logo placement
  • Multiple characters in one scene
  • Special effects and lighting
  • Social media optimized formats

3D Model Add-Ons

  • Roblox 3D Models: +25-40 Robux or +$0.25-$0.40 USD per model (decorative models I add are free!)
  • External 3D Models: +300-600 Robux or +$2-$5 USD per model (Sketchfab, custom imports, etc.)
  • Any decorative models I add to enhance the scene are included at no extra cost

Important Notes

  • Turnaround time may vary during high-demand periods
  • NSFW, offensive, or inappropriate content will NOT be accepted
  • Complex scenes with multiple external 3D models may require additional time
